Navigating the Cybersecurity Career Path by Helen Patton

Navigating the Cybersecurity Career Path by Helen Patton

Mar 19, 20224 min read

  • Non-Fiction
  • Information-Technology
  • Cybersecurity
  • Career-Development

I follow Helen Patton on LinkedIn. I think it happened after I read This Is How They Tell Me the World Ends and her review. So when Helen posted about her book on LinkedIn, I immediately bought it. It is an easy read and has practical tips for everyone - someone starting out, and also someone who is experienced.
