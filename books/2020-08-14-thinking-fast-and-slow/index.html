Thoughts

It feels awesome to have joined a bookclub to read this book. I’ve had it since the beginning of the year, read the introduction but didn’t read further yet. Reading a bit every week, discussing about it, seems like the right thing to do for a book that is about the mind and has a ton of examples and research materials. One always gets a feeling when a good thing gets started, why wasn’t I doing this so many years back?
